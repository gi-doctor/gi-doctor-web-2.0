Explore All Conditions We Treat
Gas and Bloating
Chronic gas and abdominal bloating are extremely common and can stem from SIBO, lactose malabsorption, or dietary sensitivities. Our specialized breath testing identifies the underlying cause and helps us develop an effective relief plan.
Heartburn & GERD
Persistent acid reflux, heartburn, or GERD can injure the esophagus over time. We provide precise upper endoscopy evaluation and personalized treatment to manage symptoms and protect your long-term GI health.
SIBO (Bacterial Overgrowth)
Small Intestinal Bacterial Overgrowth (SIBO) occurs when bacteria abnormally populate the small intestine. With expert hydrogen and methane breath testing, we diagnose SIBO in-office and implement highly targeted therapeutic care.
Crohn's Disease & Colitis
Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD), including Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis, requires experienced clinical care. We leverage high-resolution PillCam small bowel imaging and customized therapies to help patients regain comfort and control.
